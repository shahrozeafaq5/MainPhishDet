# Phishing Email Analysis

## Original Email

URGENT: Your bank account has been suspended.

Verify your identity immediately using this link:
https://login-verify-bank-security.com

Failure to act will permanently block your account.

## Analysis

## Phishing Analysis:

Here's a breakdown of the findings:

1. **Risk Level:** Medium

2. **Suspicious Indicators:**
   * **URL:** `https://login-verify-bank-security.com`
   * **Phrase:** "urgent"

3. **Why Suspicious:**
   * **URL:** The domain `login-verify-bank-security.com` is not a legitimate bank website. Legitimate banks use their official domain. This looks like a deliberately misleading address.
   * **Phrase:** The use of "urgent" and threats of account blockage are common tactics to pressure users into acting quickly without thinking.

4. **Recommended Action:**
   * **Do not click the link.**
   * **Independently verify:** Contact your bank directly through a known phone number or by typing their website address into your browser (do *not* use links from the email).
   * **Report:** Report this email to your bank and/or your organization's security team.



**Important Note:** A medium risk score doesn't mean this is *definitely* a phishing attempt, but it warrants extreme caution. Always prioritize verifying information through official channels.
